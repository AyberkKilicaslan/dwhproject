Project Goals


Goal/Contribution

The major goal of this study is to use the ability of young talents to predict a player’s position. Regardless of the player’s current position, machine learning models performed with the assistance of statistics from analytical teams produce a position where the player can be more efficient. As a result, the player’s potential will be better reflected, and he will be able to accomplish higher results in his future development. These studies have resulted in significant contributions to both the athlete and the team.

Project Scope

The aim of this study is to develop a model that estimates the correct positions of athletes using statistical data prepared so far. As a team, it was decided that the easiest way to do this was to use the game data of Fifa 2022, the largest football game in the world. These data are generated by monitoring football analysts of football players showing their abilities in almost all leagues of the world and testing football players with special devices in special environments. This large data set that has been created so far is at the level that it can prioritize inference for newly grown football players. The data set consists of many numeric and non-numeric values. These values consist of the physical characteristics of football players and their ability to score. At the end of the study, along with the model that will be obtained, forecasting will take place by providing the most important capabilities to the model by the user. The properties with linear relationships to each other are shown in the Confusion matrix heat map graph. When looking at the graph, defensive abilities are closely related to other defensive abilities, and offensive abilities are mostly related to other offensive abilities.
